What is a problem? A problem is not, as researchers tend to prefer, "a single, specific problem with a specific solution" or "only one answer at a time." Rather, problems are "structured in some kind of way that can provide an overall solution or solution that is feasible at any point in time, if given sufficient time, with no consequences for anyone else."
